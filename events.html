"AI Create 3.0: Connecting Pixels with Design Thinking", highlighted prompt engineering and the fusion of AI with design. Intellex -27th and 28th January, 2024

About the Event

"INTELLEX: A Critical Thinking Playground", was a technical quiz contest to challenge and enhance problem-solving, logical reasoning, and teamwork among students.
