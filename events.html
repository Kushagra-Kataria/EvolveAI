Past Events

01
HackIndia 2025 – India's Biggest Web3 & AI Hackathon - 1st and 2nd May 2025

About the Event

Hosted at Chitkara University, HackIndia 2025 brought together over 2000 developers from across India. With problem statements on Web3, Generative AI, and Fintech, the hackathon fostered open innovation, mentorship, and high-impact prototyping. Industry leaders judged groundbreaking solutions, and top teams received funding offers and incubation support.

02
L&T EduTech HackForge: Think. Innovate. Prototype. - 16th and 17th April 2025

About the Event

L&T EduTech HackForge: This high-energy 2-day hackathon began with students engaging in problem-solving challenges that demanded critical thinking, innovation, and teamwork. Participants collaborated under expert mentorship to brainstorm and prototype solutions focused on Generative AI and Agentic Tech. The event provided hands-on exposure to emerging technologies, encouraged creative solution-building, and culminated in project presentations before a panel of judges—fostering a spirit of innovation and practical learning aligned with IIC core areas.

03
INNOVISION '25 - Transforming Ideas through Research and Innovation - 25th March 2025

About the Event

INNOVISION: Transforming Ideas Through Research and Innovation was themed around Computer Vision and AI. With 25+ curated problem statements, student project showcases, and keynote talks, it promoted creativity and critical thinking. Winners received certificates, cash prizes, and project mentorship opportunities.

04
AI Create 3.0: Connecting Pixels with Design Thinking - 6th February 2025

About the Event

AI Create 3.0 challenged participants to blend AI tools with visual storytelling through prompt engineering. Three competitive rounds focused on sustainability, creativity, and replication. Industry mentor Alok Diwan guided students through building ethical and socially impactful AI content.

05
INTELLEX: A Critical Thinking Playground - 27th and 28th January 2024

About the Event

INTELLEX was a two-day technical quiz and problem-solving event designed to sharpen logic, debugging, and teamwork. Challenges included buzzer rounds, logic puzzles, and code tracing. Dr. P.K. Khosla's keynote emphasized strategy and innovation, preparing students for complex industry challenges.
